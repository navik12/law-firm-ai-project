CONFIDENTIAL — TOBACCO CASE (Cigarette Company Defense)

Client: SmokeFree Tobacco Co. (maker of "ClearLeaf" cigarettes)
Lawsuit: A long-time smoker claims ClearLeaf caused his illness.

Internal facts our side relies on:
1. Every ClearLeaf pack carries the legally required health warning.
2. The customer smoked for 30 years and also worked in a chemical plant.
3. Company funded studies say illness risk depends on many lifestyle factors.
4. All product warnings met government regulations from 1995 onward.

Defense strategy: Show the warnings were clear and other factors contributed to the illness.
