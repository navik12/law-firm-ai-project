CONFIDENTIAL — MANUFACTURING CASE (Machine Maker Defense)

Client: BuildRight Inc. (maker of the "X200 industrial press")
Lawsuit: A worker says the X200 machine crushed his hand.

Internal design facts our side relies on:
1. The X200 has TWO safety guards and an emergency stop button.
2. Inspection records show the safety guard was REMOVED by the worker's factory.
3. The user manual clearly says "never operate with guards removed."
4. The machine passed all safety certifications in 2021.

Defense strategy: Show the factory removed the safety guard, against our instructions.
