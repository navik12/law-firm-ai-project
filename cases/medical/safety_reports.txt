CONFIDENTIAL — MEDICAL CASE (Drug Company Defense)

Client: PharmaCo (maker of the drug "Reliva")
Lawsuit: A patient claims Reliva caused liver damage.

Internal safety facts our side relies on:
1. Clinical trial of 5,000 patients showed liver issues in only 0.1% of cases.
2. The drug label clearly warned about rare liver effects.
3. The patient was also taking 3 other medicines known to affect the liver.
4. FDA approved Reliva in 2019 after full safety review.

Defense strategy: Show the harm was likely caused by the other medicines, not Reliva.
